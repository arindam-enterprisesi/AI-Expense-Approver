# **Travel and Expense Policy**

## **IT Service Company - Project-Based Client Travel**

### **1\. Policy Overview**

This policy governs the reimbursement of travel and business expenses incurred by employees while executing software development projects at customer locations. All expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and directly related to business activities.

### **2\. Scope and Applicability**

This policy applies to all employees traveling to customer sites for:

- Software development and implementation projects
- System integration and deployment
- Technical consulting and support
- Client meetings and project reviews
- Training and knowledge transfer sessions

### **3\. Travel Authorization**

**Pre-Approval Required:**

- All travel must be pre-approved by the Project Manager and Department Head
- Travel exceeding 7 days requires additional approval from the Finance Director
- International travel requires CEO approval
- Emergency travel may be approved retrospectively within 48 hours

**Booking Guidelines:**

- Use company-preferred travel vendors when available
- Book flights and accommodations at least 14 days in advance for better rates
- Choose economy class for domestic flights under 4 hours
- Business class permitted for international flights over 6 hours or domestic flights over 4 hours

### **4\. Transportation Expenses**

#### **4.1 Air Travel**

- **Domestic Flights:** Economy class only
- **International Flights:** Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours
- **Booking:** Use company travel portal or approved travel agent
- **Cancellation:** Employee responsible for fees due to personal reasons
- **Upgrades:** Personal upgrades at employee's expense

#### **4.2 Ground Transportation**

- **Airport Transfers:** Taxi, ride-share, or airport shuttle
- **Car Rental:** Mid-size or compact vehicles only
  - Full insurance coverage required
  - Fuel receipts must be retained
  - Personal use of rental car not permitted
- **Public Transportation:** Buses, trains, metro - fully reimbursable
- **Personal Vehicle:** Mileage reimbursement at current IRS rate
- **Parking:** Airport parking, hotel parking, and client site parking

#### **4.3 Prohibited Transportation**

- First-class airfare (except with CEO approval)
- Luxury car rentals
- Personal vehicle use when rental car is more economical

### **5\. Accommodation Expenses**

#### **5.1 Hotel Standards**

- **Domestic:** Standard business hotels, maximum $200/night
- **International:** Maximum $300/night in major cities, $200/night elsewhere
- **Extended Stay:** For projects over 30 days, consider extended-stay hotels or corporate housing
- **Booking:** Use company-preferred hotel chains when available

#### **5.2 Accommodation Guidelines**

- Single occupancy rooms only
- Personal expenses (mini-bar, movies, spa services) not reimbursable
- Business center usage permitted for work-related activities
- Internet charges reimbursable if required for work

### **6\. Meal and Entertainment Expenses**

#### **6.1 Meal Allowances**

- **Domestic Travel:**
  - Breakfast: $25
  - Lunch: $35
  - Dinner: $55
  - Total daily maximum: $115
- **International Travel:**
  - Varies by location (refer to GSA rates or local equivalent)
  - Generally 25% higher than domestic rates

#### **6.2 Meal Guidelines**

- Receipts required for all meals over $25
- Alcohol permitted with client meals only, maximum 2 drinks
- Room service permitted when working late (after 8 PM)
- Meals included in hotel stay or flight not separately reimbursable

#### **6.3 Client Entertainment**

- Client meals and entertainment require Project Manager approval
- Maximum $150 per person for client dinners
- Alcohol permitted in moderation
- Detailed business purpose required

### **7\. Communication and Technology Expenses**

#### **7.1 Reimbursable Communication Costs**

- International roaming charges for work-related calls
- Local SIM cards or international data plans
- Hotel WiFi charges if required for work
- Conference call charges from hotel or client site

#### **7.2 Technology and Equipment**

- Laptop accessories and adapters
- Portable WiFi hotspots for project work
- Software licenses required for project delivery
- Hardware replacement due to travel damage (with incident report)

### **8\. Miscellaneous Expenses**

#### **8.1 Reimbursable Expenses**

- Visa and passport fees for business travel
- Travel insurance (when required)
- Baggage fees for business equipment
- Laundry and dry cleaning (trips over 5 days)
- Tips and gratuities (within reasonable limits)
- Currency conversion fees
- Client site parking fees
- Business supplies and materials

#### **8.2 Non-Reimbursable Expenses**

- Personal entertainment and recreation
- Personal shopping and gifts
- Upgrades for personal comfort
- Expenses for family members
- Lost or stolen personal items
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Personal insurance
- Personal phone calls and internet usage

### **9\. Expense Reporting and Documentation**

#### **9.1 Submission Requirements**

- Submit expense reports within 30 days of trip completion
- Use company expense management system
- Attach all original receipts
- Provide detailed business purpose for each expense
- Include client/project code for proper cost allocation

#### **9.2 Required Documentation**

- **Receipts:** All expenses over $25 require receipts
- **Mileage:** Odometer readings and business purpose
- **Client Meals:** List of attendees and business purpose
- **Foreign Currency:** Original receipts with conversion rates
- **Credit Card Statements:** If original receipts are lost

#### **9.3 Approval Process**

- Project Manager reviews and approves
- Finance team validates against policy
- Department Head final approval for expenses over $1,000
- Payment processed within 14 days of approval

### **10\. Advance Payments and Corporate Cards**

#### **10.1 Travel Advances**

- Available for trips over $1,000 in estimated expenses
- Request 10 days before travel
- Reconciliation required within 30 days of return
- Outstanding advances deducted from final paycheck if not reconciled

#### **10.2 Corporate Credit Cards**

- Available for senior consultants and project managers
- Personal use prohibited
- Monthly reconciliation required
- All receipts must be submitted regardless of amount

### **11\. Compliance and Audit**

#### **11.1 Policy Compliance**

- All expenses subject to audit
- False claims may result in disciplinary action
- Repeated policy violations may affect travel privileges
- Finance department may request additional documentation

#### **11.2 Currency and Tax Considerations**

- All expenses reported in company base currency
- VAT/GST recovery handled by finance department
- Tax implications of international travel communicated separately
- Expense reports may be used for tax and compliance purposes

### **12\. Special Circumstances**

#### **12.1 Extended Projects (Over 30 Days)**

- Monthly return trip home may be reimbursed
- Consider temporary relocation allowance
- Extended stay accommodation rates negotiated
- Equipment shipping costs covered

#### **12.2 Emergency Situations**

- Medical emergencies: All reasonable expenses covered
- Travel delays: Meals and accommodation covered
- Natural disasters: Evacuation
